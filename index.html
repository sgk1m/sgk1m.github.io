Cryptographer

Seongkwang Kim

Senior Engineer at Samsung SDS

I am a cryptography researcher at Samsung SDS. From March 2026, I will join Graduate School of Cybersecurity, Korea University as an assistant professor. I received my Ph.D. in information security from KAIST in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Jooyoung Lee. My research interests include symmetric primitive design, privacy-enhancing technologies (MPC, ZKP), and post-quantum signature schemes.
