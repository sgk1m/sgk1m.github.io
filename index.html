About me

I am a senior engineer in Security Algorithm Lab at Samsung SDS. I am currently interested in private enhancing technologies, design of symmetric ciphers, and signature based on zero-knowledge proof.

I obtained my B.S. degree in Mathematics at POSTECH, Pohang, M.S. degree in Mathematical Sciences at KAIST, Daejeon, and Ph.D. degree in Graduate School of Information Security (GSIS) at KAIST, Daejeon. My advisor for M.S. degree was Prof. Sanggeun Han, and My advisor for Ph.D. degree was Prof. Jooyoung Lee.
