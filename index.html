About me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Graduate School of Information Security (GSIS) at KAIST, Daejeon, Korea. My research interest is on provable security of symmetric key cryptography, design of symmetric key cryptography, and homomorphic encryption. Currently I am specifically interested in transciphering in homomorphic encryption and design of HE-friendly ciphers.

I obtained my B.S degree in mathematics from POSTECH, Pohang, in 2016, and M.S. degree in mathematical sciences from KAIST, Daejeon. My advisor for M.S. degree was Prof. Sanggeun Han, and My advisor Ph.D. degree is Prof. Jooyoung Lee.
